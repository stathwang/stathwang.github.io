about

Hi I'm Seong Hyun. I'm a Data Scientist, Statistician, and Machine Learning Engineer based in New York.

Previously, I worked at HonestFund, an online marketplace lending startup in Seoul, Korea, and at CogoLabs, an adtech and programmatic marketing startup accelerator in Cambridge, Massachusetts.

I have a master's degree in Statistics from Yale, and I studied Statistics and Economics at Harvard where I received my bachelor's degree. I wrote a thesis advised by Professor Joseph Blitzstein in which I proposed and evaluated a novel algorithm SlopeUnion to recommend products in online social ratings network.

research interest

  • Probabilistic Modeling, Machine Learning, and Bayesian Inference
  • Natural Language Processing and Deep Learning
  • Swarm Intelligence and Nature-Inspired Optimization Algorithms
  • Recommender Systems

Philosophically, I'm a Bayesian in principle, a likelihoodist in practice, and a frequentist in public. I like traveling so I've backpacked through Europe, visited Korea, Japan, China, Costa Rica, and parts of the United States. I also love sushi, especially raw salmon, and like taking random photos of nature and daily life. I don't like cilantro though.

I launched my blog in early 2015 with the site domain thedatalogical, later merged with my Github account stathwang in 2016 under .io extension. The old site is deprecated and no longer maintained. I try to keep my blog updated occasionally with posts about interesting data science projects and visualizations I've done as well as share my thoughts on just about anything. All opinions are my own. I also wish to do some work on my discontinued music producing.
